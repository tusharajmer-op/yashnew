Our Belief

We believe by attracting clients and patients with similar beliefs or interests, the therapeutic rapport will be stronger and thus the healing will be more effective. WHY

During the tumultuous events of 2020, we came up with the idea of starting a platform that would help both healthcare professionals and potential patients find each other by way of how interests and values, and not just by “are they in-network?” For far too long, healthcare professionals have been taught to perform as sterile automatons without any strong identifications of how they might identify out of the possibility it might drive a wedge in the therapeutic relationship between the provider and the patient.

THE PLATFORM

Visionaries

As the visionaries and co-founders of “MyKindaDoc” we strive to create two unique ways for helping others. The first being a platform where healthcare workers and healers such as ourselves can proudly and openly market our beliefs as it is fundamental to our being. Secondly, we are noticing that more and more individuals in the public would be more likely to engage in their health if they felt they shared similar views and beliefs with their healthcare providers.

That’s why we have envisioned a platform that could become a place where the public can search for health care providers that match up with the desired specialty, accepted forms of payment, location, etc. they are looking for – but just as importantly – to also have the capability to filter for those who share similar interests and beliefs. We believe that this will drastically increase healthy outcomes that our nation so desperately craves and needs.
